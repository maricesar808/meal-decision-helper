# Mari's Meal Decision Helper

## What the tool does

This tool helps me decide what or where to eat. Add meal or restaurant options, rate each one by cost, healthiness, and travel time, then calculate top three choices.

## How to use it

1. Enter a meal or restaurant name.
2. Rate its cost, healthiness, and travel time from 1–5. Need at least two options.
3. Click **Calculate** to see your top choices.
4. Use **Random Pick** if you still cannot decide.
